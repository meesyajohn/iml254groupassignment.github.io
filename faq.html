1. What types of clothing does Bushbubbs Clothing offer for women?

Bushbubbs Clothing offers a wide range of women's apparel including dresses, tops, bottoms, outerwear, activewear, and accessories.

2. What sizes are available at Bushbubbs Clothing for women?

Our women's clothing is freesize.

3. How can I place an order?

Orders can be placed directly through our website. Simply select the items you wish to purchase, choose your size and color preferences, add to cart and proceed to fill in the form to checkout.

4. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards, Online Transfer and Apple Pay.

5. How can I track my order?

After your order is placed, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this number on our website to track the status of your shipment.

6. What is your return policy?

We offer a 7-day return policy for unworn, unwashed items with original tags attached. To initiate a return, please visit our Returns page and follow the instructions provided.

7. Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we offer international shipping to a number of countries. Shipping rates and delivery times vary depending on the destination.

8. How can I contact customer service?

Our customer service team can be reached via email at support@bushbubbsclothingsdnbhd.com or by phone at 0172340518. We are available Monday through Friday from 9 AM to 6 PM.

9. Are your products sustainably made?

Yes, Bushbubbs Clothing is committed to sustainability. We use eco-friendly materials and ethical manufacturing practices to ensure our products are both high-quality and environmentally responsible.

10. Do you have a physical store location?

Currently, Bushbubbs Clothing operates exclusively online. However, we do participate in pop-up shops and events throughout the year, which we announce on our website and social media channels.
